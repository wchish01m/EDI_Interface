Welcome to the EDI Interface!

Below is a brief overview of the navigation options.

  • Home: This is your home page. So selecting the Home button will take you back here!
  • Facilities: The Facilities dropdown menu will allow you to change the results each table displays according to the facility selected.
  • Shipping: The Shipping dropdown menu will give you access to all tables containing shipping information.
    • Results: The Results button will take you to the table that shows the most recent shipping export results.
    • Label Audit: The Label Audit button will take you to a page which will allow you to search for label information based off of specific fields.
  • Sales: The Sales button will take you the table that shows sales requirements.
  • Intranet: The Intranet button will take you back to Topre's intranet page.
